Happy Clients

I am a museum curator. I got to know MuHu one year ago and I started following them. After a couple of months, I decided to call them to present them my museum and see if they had nice ideas to improve users’ experience. I could not have done anything better! All the staff, from the developers to the storytellers will follow your project step by step and the final result was amazing! I noticed a considerable increase of users visiting and enjoying my museum. Highly recommended!

Robert Radford

Museum Curator

I teach Spanish language at linguistic high school. I love museums, but my students do not… well… I should say “did not”. All the classes I brought to a museum whose app was realized by MuHu were exceptionally enthusiast. They all downloaded the app and enjoyed the museum visit. I am sure they both learn new things and lived an amusing experience.

Ross Wyman

Teacher

I am the parent of two young teenagers. They spend their free time using their smartphones, so I told myself: “why should not I take advantage of it?” I brought them to visit a museum for which MuHu created an interactive app. I have never seen them so happy of visiting a museum and learning some culture. If you have kids and you want them to learn something while enjoying a museum, you should take a look at the lists of museums which are provided with MuHu’s apps.

Roger Scott

Father

I was not too convinced, either. But when we started following the story created by MuHu’s developers, everything changed. My brother loved the experience of the app, but, especially, he loved the exhibition!
